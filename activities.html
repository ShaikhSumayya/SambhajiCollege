Activities

On The Occassion Of 4th National Pharmacovigilance Week
  • 27 September
  • Nanded Bypass Road Nanded
  • 12:06 PM

National Pharmacovigilance Week is a significant event that focuses on raising awareness about the safety of medicines and the importance of reporting adverse drug reactions (ADRs). The initiative aims to promote the culture of pharmacovigilance, ensuring that medicines are used safely and effectively, benefiting public health at large.

Raising Awareness About Medication Side Effects
  • 27 September
  • Sahyadri Nagar Nanded
  • 5:30 PM

Raising awareness about medication side effects is a shared responsibility between healthcare professionals, regulatory agencies, and patients. Empowering individuals with knowledge leads to safer medication practices, improved health outcomes, and a more proactive approach to personal well-being. Every effort in spreading awareness can contribute to a safer healthcare environment.

World Pharmacist Day
  • 25 September
  • Babapur Maharashtra India
  • 5:30 PM

World Pharmacist Day is celebrated annually on September 25 to recognize and honor the invaluable role of pharmacists in healthcare systems across the globe. This day, established by the International Pharmaceutical Federation (FIP), aims to highlight the contributions of pharmacists to improving global health and emphasize their commitment to ensuring safe and effective medication use.
