About Shri Sambhaji Foundation

Shri Sambhaji Foundation is committed to excellence in education, focusing on Pharmacy, Management, and Technology. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, innovation, and leadership skills to meet the evolving demands of the modern world. By fostering a dynamic learning environment and industry partnerships, we prepare future-ready professionals dedicated to making a positive impact on society.

Who WE ARE

Raise India Foundation stands as a pillar of support, dedicated to uplifting communities and driving positive change throughout India. We are a nationally registered non-profit organization focused on key areas such as education, healthcare, livelihoods, and environmental conservation, addressing pressing challenges with innovative and sustainable solutions.

Our commitment to integrity, compassion, and collaboration guides every initiative, ensuring that our efforts create lasting impact and promote inclusive growth. Together, we strive to build a brighter future for every individual, one where opportunity and dignity are accessible to all.
